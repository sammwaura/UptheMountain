During their discussion, they noted that it would be an occasion to hoist the flag again at the top of Mt Kenya. This triggered the feeling that, the Church could recognize the hand of God during this period and mark the occasion by placing the Bible at the top of the mountain at point Lenana.
Each one of them was tasked to sell the idea to their friends and the leadership of their respective churches at were Deliverance Umoja and PCEA Zimmerman to support the initiative. The initial members of Up The Mountain Bible Mission (UTMBM) were drawn from the two congregations. The recruitment process brought additional members from other denominations such as the Catholic church, ACK and CITAM.

Our Mission to take the bible to the top of Mt Kenya

The planning for the mission started in June 2013 through consultation meetings held at Clarion Hotel, in Nairobi.
